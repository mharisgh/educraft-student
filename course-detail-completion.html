Introduction to 3D Modeling and Animation

This course will take you through the fundamentals of 3D modeling and animation, providing you with the skills to create realistic 3D scenes and animations using industry-standard tools. Whether you are a beginner or have some experience, this course will enhance your ability to work in a 3D environment.

What you will learn

Understand the basics of 3D modeling using Blender.
Learn how to detail models to add realism and depth.
Master lighting techniques to enhance the visual appeal of your scenes.
Create basic animations to bring your models to life.

Prerequisites

• No prior experience in 3D modeling or animation is required.

• Basic knowledge of computer operations is recommended.

• An interest in creative and technical aspects of 3D design.

• Willingness to explore and experiment with 3D tools and techniques.
